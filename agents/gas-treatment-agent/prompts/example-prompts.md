# Example Prompts

These prompts use public, synthetic data only. They are intended for early-stage screening.

## Water-Content and Dehydration Screening

```text
Use public synthetic data to screen a natural gas at 35 degC and 70 bar against a sales-gas water spec of 32 mg/Sm^3. Estimate the saturated water content, indicate whether dehydration appears required, report the warning level, and suggest validated GPSA and NeqSim workflows.
```

## Compression Power Screening

```text
Use public synthetic data to screen a single compression stage with a suction pressure of 30 bar, a discharge pressure of 70 bar, a suction temperature of 35 degC, a polytropic efficiency of 0.75, a molecular weight of 18 g/mol, and a flow rate of 5 kg/s. Report the polytropic head, the discharge temperature, the gas power, and the warning level, and suggest validated API 617, API 619, and NeqSim workflows.
```

## Combined Gas Treatment Screening

```text
Screen a gas treatment train using public synthetic data. Summarize the saturated water content against the sales-gas water spec, indicate whether dehydration appears required, and estimate the polytropic head, discharge temperature, and gas power for a single compression stage. List the required follow-up studies and prepare a screening report outline with assumptions, limitations, and a human review checklist.
```
