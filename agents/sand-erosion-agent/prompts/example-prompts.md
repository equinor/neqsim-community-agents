# Example Prompts

These prompts illustrate how to use the Sand Erosion Agent with public, synthetic, or approved data only. The agent performs early-stage screening and always requires qualified human review.

## Erosional Velocity and Remaining Wall Life from Known Conditions

```text
Screen a sand-producing flowline for erosional velocity and remaining wall life. The flow velocity is 12 m/s, the mixture density is 120 kg/m3, the pipe diameter is 0.25 m, the wall thickness is 12.7 mm, the sand rate is 50 kg/day, the corrosion allowance is 3 mm, and the design life is 25 years. Report the erosional velocity, velocity ratio, screening erosion rate, remaining wall, and remaining wall life, and recommend validated NeqSim erosion and pipe-flow workflows.
```

## Low-Sand Compliant Check

```text
Using public synthetic data, screen a line that runs at a velocity ratio of about 0.6 with a low sand rate. Confirm whether the remaining wall life appears to exceed the design life and report the verdict.
```

## Required Wall Life Check

```text
Screen a sand-producing case against a required remaining wall life equal to the design life. Flag the case if the estimated remaining wall life appears insufficient, list the required follow-up erosion and inspection studies, and prepare a sand-erosion screening report outline with clear assumptions, limitations, and a human review checklist.
```

## Report Outline

```text
Prepare a transparent sand-erosion screening report outline based on the erosional-velocity and remaining-wall-life indicators. Separate facts, assumptions, and recommendations, and state that the results are educational placeholders that do not replace qualified integrity review.
```
